The team

We are a 14-member committee from EPFL, supervised by the mathematics faculty professor Nicolas Monod, each bringing unique expertise to the table. Our backgrounds encompass a wide spectrum, from mathematics to computer science, through physics and electronics, ensuring a holistic approach to problem-solving. This diversity is our strength, enabling us to tackle multifaceted challenges with creativity and precision.
