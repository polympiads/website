IMC 2024

The International Mathematics Competition (IMC) for University Students is an annual mathematics competition open to all undergraduate students of mathematics.

Read More

While the IMO (International Mathematics Olympiad) is focused on high school students, the IMC (International Mathematics Competition) is for university-level students. It is considered one of the most prestigious international math competitions for those studying mathematics or related fields at the university level, where 70 teams representing the highest-ranked universities in the world compete against each other for two days.

Our goal this year is to send another team of 10 students to the 31st IMC in July 2024.
