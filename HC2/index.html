HC² 2024

The Helvetic coding contest known as HC² is the biggest programming contest in Switzerland. Held over one day on the EPFL campus, it attracts over 200 participants with a passion for programming. After 4 years of absence, this year for the first time, the Polympiads association is organizing the event on Saturday April 13, 2024.

HC² is an occasion for students to collaborate, showcase their problem-solving skills, and create lasting memories.

  • Date: Saturday 13/04/2024
  • Time: 10:30 AM - 19:00 PM
  • Where: CO buidling, level 0, EPFL
