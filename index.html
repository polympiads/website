Who are we ?

Polympiads is a dynamic association founded by EPFL students with a passion for promoting disciplines such as mathematics and programming. We believe in the power of challenging oneself to inspire creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. We organize weekly training sessions, hosted in the Bernoulli center, where we bring together the most talented and competent students of the campus in order to participate in both national and international competitions.
